Hobbies & Interests

Beyond the code.

I've always believed that being a well-rounded person makes you a better developer. These are the things that keep me curious, grounded, and inspired.

What I Love

🍵

Tea

A proper cup of tea is one of life's simple pleasures. I'm particularly fond of high-quality oolong and aged pu-erh. There's something meditative about the ritual.

📚

Reading

I read broadly—technical books, philosophy, history, and the occasional novel. Lately I've been interested in the intersection of AI and human cognition.

✈️

Travel

Speaking at conferences has taken me to wonderful places. I particularly love discovering local tea cultures and bookstores wherever I go.

🎨

Design

I care deeply about aesthetics and user experience. Good design is invisible— it just feels right. I draw inspiration from architecture, typography, and products that get the details right.

🔒

Privacy & Local-First

More than a hobby, this is a passion. I believe in tools that respect your data and work offline. The future I want to build is one where you own your digital life.

🌍

Open Source

Contributing to the commons is deeply rewarding. I've been maintaining open source projects for over a decade, and I learn something new from the community every day.
