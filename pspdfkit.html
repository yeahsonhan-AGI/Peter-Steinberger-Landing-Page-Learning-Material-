PSPDFKit

In 2010, while working as an iOS developer in Vienna, Peter noticed a fundamental problem: PDFs were incredibly difficult to handle on mobile devices. This frustration became the seed for PSPDFKit.

The Beginning

Founded alongside co-founder Martin Schürrer, PSPDFKit was bootstrapped from Peter's apartment in Vienna with no external funding. The goal was simple but ambitious: bring magazines and documents to iPhones by solving mobile PDF rendering issues.

What started as a solution to a personal problem grew into a global B2B document solutions company.

Growth & Exit

Over 13 years, PSPDFKit grew to more than 60 employees, serving customers worldwide with a developer-first approach to PDF SDKs. The company became the go-to solution for mobile PDF rendering, used by countless apps and enterprises.

Peter successfully exited PSPDFKit in a deal reported to be worth approximately $100 million — a remarkable outcome for a bootstrapped European company.

The Bootstrapping Philosophy

Peter has been vocal about the benefits of bootstrapping over venture capital. By maintaining full control and focusing on sustainable growth, he built PSPDFKit into a profitable business without diluting his vision or answering to external investors.

This philosophy continues to inform his approach to building — prioritizing product, user needs, and sustainable growth over hype-driven expansion.
