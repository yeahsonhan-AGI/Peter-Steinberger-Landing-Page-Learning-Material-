OpenAI

Following the success and subsequent sale of PSPDFKit, and his pioneering work on OpenClaw, Peter Steinberger joined OpenAI to work on what may be the most important computing platform of our time.

The Next Generation

At OpenAI, Peter is focused on next-generation personal agents — AI systems that can act autonomously on behalf of users, understanding context, making decisions, and executing complex tasks.

This role builds directly on his experience with OpenClaw, where he developed a vision for AI agents that prioritize user privacy and local execution. Now, he brings that philosophy to one of the world's leading AI companies.

Vision

Peter has been vocal about the transformative potential of AI agents. He sees a future where coding agents consume 80-90% fewer tokens through optimization, and where AI assistants can handle increasingly sophisticated tasks with minimal human intervention.

His approach combines technical pragmatism — honed over years of building production software — with an optimistic view of AI's potential to augment human capability.

AI Development Workflow

Peter practices what he preaches. His development workflow is deeply integrated with AI coding assistants, documenting his approach in posts like "My Current AI Dev Workflow" and sharing live coding sessions exploring AI-powered development.

This hands-on experience with AI tools gives him unique insight into both their capabilities and their limitations — insight that now informs product development at OpenAI.

Media & Public Appearances

Peter has appeared on the Lex Fridman Podcast, discussing OpenClaw, AI agents, and his development philosophy. These conversations offer a window into his thinking about the future of AI and personal computing.
